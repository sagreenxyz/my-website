# Types of Tissues in the Human Body

**Module 1: Introduction to Tissues**

Defining a Tissue:
* What is a tissue in the biological context?
* What are the two main components of a tissue?
* What are the four primary types of tissues found in the human body?

Overview of Tissue Functions:
* Briefly describe the major roles of each of the four tissue types:
    * Epithelial tissue
    * Connective tissue
    * Muscle tissue

**Module 2: Epithelial Tissue - Covering and Lining**

General Characteristics of Epithelial Tissue:
* List and explain the key characteristics of epithelial tissues (cellularity, specialized contacts, polarity, support, avascularity, innervation, regeneration).
* What are the primary functions of covering and lining epithelia?

Classification of Covering and Lining Epithelia:
* Explain the criteria used to classify covering and lining epithelia (cell shape and number of layers).
* Describe the following types of covering and lining epithelia, including their structure, function, and typical locations:
    * Simple Squamous Epithelium
    * Simple Cuboidal Epithelium
    * Simple Columnar Epithelium
    * Pseudostratified Columnar Epithelium
    * Stratified Squamous Epithelium (Keratinized and Nonkeratinized)
    * Stratified Cuboidal Epithelium
    * Stratified Columnar Epithelium
    * Transitional Epithelium
